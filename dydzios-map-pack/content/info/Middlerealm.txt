Middlerealm

Map for shadow of death

Author: Mikael wallin, Sweden

Map size: large, with a little bit underground
Map difficulty: Expert
Map special win: Conquer all
Map special loss: Lose all

Description:
A balanced,detailed map fitted for competitive play with at most 6 players.
All players starts equal, but the fortress can get to its second town faster than the others, therefore getting a little advantage.

Enjoy!(and please mail me your opinions!)
